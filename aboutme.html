Who is Vidhu?

I am Vidhu Mon TV, a passionate artist and illustrator dedicated to crafting visual narratives that captivate the imagination. My artistic journey began at the prestigious Raja Ravivarma College of Pine Arts, Mavelikkara, where I graduated in 2021 with a specialization in Applied Arts. Following my insatiable desire to delve deeper into the world of visual storytelling, I pursued post-graduation in Applied Arts at RLV College of Music and Fine Arts, Tripunithura

While my academic foundation is rooted in applied arts, my heart beats for the intricate realm of illustration. I find immense joy and purpose in breathing life into ideas, characters, and worlds through my art.With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, I have honed my skills as an illustrator, comic artist,and concept artist.

My artistic philosophy is anchored in the belief that every stroke of the brush or click of the digital pen holds the potential to tell a unique story. Whether I am crafting whimsical illustrations, dynamic comic panels, or conceptualizing immersive worlds, my focus is to transport viewers into realms where imagination knows no bounds.

Beyond the strokes and pixels, I am committed to sharing my knowledge and passion with others. Teaching is not just a profession but a calling, and I take pride in guiding fellow artists on their own creative journeys. Join me as we explore the infinite possibilities of illustration and storytelling together.

Thank you for being a part of my artistic odyssey. Let's enter on this creative adventure where every line drawn is a step into a world of boundless imagination.
